What is SolScope?

SolScope is a free Token Intelligence Terminal for the Solana blockchain. Paste any token address and get a complete intelligence report in seconds — no wallet connection, no sign-up, no fees. Every report combines six on-chain risk signals, a lifecycle stage classifier, a 24-hour price chart, and a plain-English verdict that anyone can understand.

Built on top of the Birdeye Data API — the same data infrastructure trusted by Phantom, Raydium, Coinbase, and Bybit.

Risk Scoring — 6 signals

Every token is scored from 0 (safest) to 100 (most dangerous) by evaluating six independent on-chain signals. The scores are additive and capped at 100.

SignalMax PointsWhat it detects
Liquidity depth25 ptsLow liquidity = easy price manipulation
Volume / Market Cap ratio25 ptsExtreme ratio = coordinated pump signal
24h price spike20 pts200%+ move = possible pump in progress
Holder count20 ptsUnder 50 holders = concentrated dump risk
Wash trading signal15 ptsHigh volume + very few trades = fake activity
Unique wallet activity10 ptsFew wallets driving high volume = coordinated buying
Lifecycle Stages

Every token sits somewhere in a market cycle. Understanding where saves money. SolScope classifies tokens into five stages based on volume trends, price momentum, and holder velocity.

StageWhat it means
EarlyNew launch, very few holders, trajectory unknown. Highest risk, highest potential.
GrowingVolume rising organically, holder count building. Typically the best entry window.
PeakingPrice has already spiked significantly. Late entry risk — momentum buying phase.
DistributionPrice stalling while volume fades. Early holders selling to late buyers. Classic dump setup.
DecliningBoth price and volume falling. Interest fading. High risk of further downside.
Data Sources

All data is sourced from the Birdeye Data API in real time. Endpoints used: /defi/token_overview (price, volume, holders, liquidity), /defi/ohlcv (price history), /defi/token_trending (trending status), and /defi/tokenlist (comparable tokens).
